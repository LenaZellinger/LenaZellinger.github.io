I'm an [ELLIS](https://ellis.eu/) PhD student at the University of Edinburgh, supervised by [Antonio Vergari](http://nolovedeeplearning.com/), [Esmeralda S. Whitammer](https://malkin1729.github.io/) and [Vincent Fortuin](https://fortuin.github.io/). 
I'm broadly interested in approximate Bayesian inference and uncertainty quantification. Currently, I'm mostly working on variational inference (VI), aiming to derive [formal guarantees for when VI will successfully recover certain characteristics of the target distribution](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.21407).
If you are excited about similar topics, [reach out](mailto:L.Zellinger@sms.ed.ac.uk)!
